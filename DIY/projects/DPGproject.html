DIY Digital Signal Generator

Description

A simple digital pattern generator using two CMOS IC chips. This can be done just on a breadboard, or worked out into a eurorack module or stand alone unit.
You'll wanna use a bipolar power source, if you dont have one, you use two 9volt watteries connected like this

Materials

    IC's

  • x1 4516
  • x2 4013
  • x5 OP Amps, your choice!
  • x6 4069 Hex Inverter

    Resistors

  • x6 1K
  • x2 10k
  • x1 20k
  • x4 100k
  • x2 1M

Potentiometers

  • x1 5M
  • x5 50k
  • x1 10k

Capacitors

  • x1 1uF
  • x1 0.1uF
  • x1 270pF
  • x1 0.47uF

    • Miscellaneous

  • 4x 1N4198
  • 1x 9V battery clip
  • 1x SPST Switch
  • 1x 9V battery
  • 1x 1/4 inch or 3.5mm audio jack
