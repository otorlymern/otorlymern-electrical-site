Vocal Synthesis

Description

This is a fun project/patch you can do with some basic modules or a competent enough fixed architechture (non-modular) synth. THis could even be implemented in max or pure data. This patch shows how the basic principles of human speech work, teaches about resonances, formants, and the importance of filters. With a couple filters tuned in the right range, you can eerily but effectivly mimic the human voice enunciating vowels. It has to be heard to understood. I rermeber when i first got the Wiard Dual Borg filter i would run it in parallel with any ofther filters i had with it in parallel mode, giving me like 4 four filters, and starts sweeping around in bandpass mode until i found that creepy sweet spot. Its lots of fun. Just read the atcile and patch based on the diagram and you should be good.
